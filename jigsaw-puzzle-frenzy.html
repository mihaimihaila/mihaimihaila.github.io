Jigsaw Puzzle Frenzy

Jigsaw Puzzle Frenzy features carefully selected puzzles of landmarks, architecture, landscapes, plants and animals. A community of 30.000 players participate in the Puzzle of the Day competition and play over 1000+ jigsaw puzzles at their own pace.

  • What makes the game unique is the competition against other players
  • The game contains a new puzzle every day, with players competing to get rewards daily
  • The game contains stunning pictures of European landmarks, landscapes, food, plants, animals and architecture
  • The puzzles can be played in three unique jigsaw shapes and up to 300 pieces
  • You pick the shape and the number of pieces, depending on how experienced you feel
